# 中文摘要

图像恢复是计算机视觉的基础性课题，面向复杂退化场景时，现有方法因缺乏区域级退化建模能力而难以实现差异化处理。针对退化空间异质性表征不足这一关键科学问题，本项目提出"通过显式建模退化空间分布，多智能体协同策略可显著提升异质退化场景的恢复质量"的科学假说。研究内容包括：（1）构建空间感知退化分析机制，利用视觉语言模型实现区域级退化识别与空间退化图生成；（2）构建层级多智能体协同规划机制，通过全局调度器与区域专家协同实现工具链选择与参数优化；（3）构建基于轨迹蒸馏的轻量路由网络，将复杂推理知识压缩为可部署的轻量决策模型。本研究将推动图像恢复从全局统一表征向区域级动态表征演进，为复杂场景视觉感知提供理论与方法支撑。

# English Abstract

Image restoration is a fundamental task in computer vision. Existing methods struggle to achieve differentiated processing in complex degradation scenarios due to the lack of regional degradation modeling capabilities. Addressing the key scientific problem of insufficient characterization of spatial degradation heterogeneity, this project proposes the hypothesis that "multi-agent collaborative strategies can significantly improve restoration quality in heterogeneous degradation scenarios through explicit modeling of spatial degradation distribution". The research includes: (1) constructing a spatially-aware degradation analysis mechanism that utilizes visual language models for regional degradation identification and spatial degradation map generation; (2) constructing a hierarchical multi-agent collaborative planning mechanism that enables tool chain selection and parameter optimization through coordination between global scheduler and regional experts; (3) constructing a lightweight routing network based on trajectory distillation that compresses complex reasoning knowledge into deployable lightweight decision models. This research will advance image restoration from global uniform representation to regional dynamic representation, providing theoretical and methodological support for visual perception in complex scenarios.
